<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

The **Global EV Market Analysis Dashboard** aims to explore the worldwide growth and adoption of electric vehicles.  
Using **Power BI**, this project visualizes EV sales, stock, and technology trends across countries â€” helping understand how innovation, infrastructure, and sustainability policies are shaping the future of transportation.

---

<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-objective"></a>Business Objective</h2>

The main goals of this project are to:
- Analyze **global EV sales and stock trends** across different regions.  
- Identify **top-performing countries** and **vehicle categories** in EV adoption.

- Provide **data-driven insights** to support the transition toward sustainable mobility.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="key-insights"></a>Key Insights</h2>

1. **China** leads the global EV market, contributing the highest sales and stock share.  
2. **Passenger vehicles** dominate EV adoption worldwide.  
3. **BEVs** are more widely adopted than **PHEVs**.  
4. Global EV stock shows **consistent year-over-year growth**, especially post-2018.  
5. **Developing nations** show promising EV growth backed by supportive policies.
